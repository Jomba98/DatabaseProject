Project Overview

This website shows visual representation of the number of heart disease deaths and the median income of each state in the United States in 2017, as well as the Medicare enrollment in each state from the previous year (2016).

Motivation

With Medicare becoming such a hot-button issue in the United States, we wanted to explore how many Americans are enrolled in Medicare. We also took into account the median income and heart disease deaths of each state to see if there is a correlation. Meaning, we wanted to see if a state with a low median income and Medicare enrollment also had a high heart disease death rate in the following year. Our purpose is to increase awareness about these issues so they may someday be addressed.
